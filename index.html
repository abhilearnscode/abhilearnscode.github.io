Varsha Prasad

About the artist

Varsha prasad is a lazy painter and a lover of dogs, fishes, and cats. She has sailed the seven seas and has set foot on every continent, except Australia when she was in the merchant navy.

She paints, cooks, and makes all kinds of handicrafts because she loves colors, shadows, and lights. Their intermittent play. The hide and seek of emotions. Indoor and outdoor. Matter and energy. Transformation and metamorphosis.

She knows the difference between smell, fragrance, and aroma.

She is a shy writer too. Almost a secluded recluse. She writes too far and too less in between but when she writes, she engages your heart and burns the truth.

What you can buy/gift

Paintings

She paints mainly using pastels and watercolors. She loves abstracts, zentangles, portraits, landscapes, and dreamscapes.

Handicrafts

She is equally good at quilling, papier mache crafts, Best-out-of-waste crafts, and clay modelling.

Handmade greeting cards

Social media has connected us to many loved ones but in the process has disconnected our love. Don’t you want to bring back that personal touch? That new book fragrance in your messages and wishes to your loved ones on special occasions? Get unique, exclusive handmade greeting cards painted with the right words and pictures to convey what you want. Straight to the heart.

Portraits & abstracts

Primarily, her motifs range from nature to the natural... But she loves painting women in all their moods, phases, shapes, sizes, faces, grime, and glory... She paints life on to portraits while her abstracts breathe life on to dreams.

Doodles & sketches

Her art finds inspiration anywhere, anytime. From Requiem for a Dream to a Charagh Din shirt worn by her dad, she gets inspired and she paints. When she paints, she becomes one with oil pastels and acrylic colors on canvas till the art engulfs its artist.
